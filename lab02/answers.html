  1. Question 1: Based on a web search but in your own words, describe what purpose the doctype line has. Gives the browser information on what type of document is being read
  2. Question 2: What happens when you omit the doctype? What is the name of the mode that the browser enters? (It starts with a Q.) Quirks Mode
  3. Question 3: What happens when you place two HTML elements as siblings? By siblings I mean the two elements sit at the same level of the hierarchy. One opens right after the other closes. Nothing happens, there is nothing between the tags.
  4. Question 4. What is currently displayed in your browser’s tab when viewing answers.html? 127.0.0.1:7458/lab02/answers.html
  5. Question 5: How does the browser handle the second body element? It puts it in with the rest of the body content at the front.
  6. Question 6: What does the browser do to make the hierarchy of headers clearly visible to the viewer? There is a space between them
  7. Question 7: What does your list look like immediately after you embed it in an ol element? Indented and all in one paragraph.
  8. Question 8: Name two lists of information whose orders don’t matter. For example: the ingredients list of a recipe. Names of people in a class, grocery list
